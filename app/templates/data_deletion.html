User Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with our application, Smartbreed.io, you can do so by following the steps outlined below:

1. Email Request

Send an email to our support team at support@smartbreed.io with the subject line "Request for Data Deletion". Include your user identification details and any other relevant information that will help us identify your account in our system.

2. Verification

Once we receive your request, we will contact you to verify your identity to prevent unauthorized access and deletion of your account. You may be asked to provide additional details to confirm the ownership of the account.

3. Processing

After verifying your identity, we will proceed with the deletion of your data from our databases. Please note that some information might remain in our archives for a certain period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, but it will not be active.

4. Confirmation

We will confirm the deletion of your data via email once the process is complete.

Please note that this process will delete your data from the Smartbreed.io application and will not affect your data on Facebook. To manage or delete your data on Facebook, please refer to the Facebook settings and their privacy policies.
