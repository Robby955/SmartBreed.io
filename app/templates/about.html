About Us - Smart Dog Breed Classifier

Welcome to the Smart Dog Breed Classifier

Our mission is to create the most accurate and user-friendly dog breed classification tool available online. By leveraging cutting-edge machine learning techniques and community engagement, we aim to continually improve our model and provide a fun and educational experience for dog enthusiasts and owners alike.

Our Technology

At the core of our application is a deep learning model trained on thousands of images across various dog breeds. Utilizing transfer learning with a MobileNetV2 architecture, we've fine-tuned our model to achieve remarkable accuracy in breed identification. Moreover, we store user-submitted images securely on the cloud, contributing to an ever-growing dataset that enhances our model's performance.

Community Driven

Our platform is not just about breed identification; it's a collaborative project that invites users to participate in the learning process. By playing our "Guess the Breed" game or correcting identified breeds, users contribute valuable data that helps refine our algorithms. It's a community effort to build the most comprehensive and accurate dog breed classifier available.

Future Endeavors

Looking ahead, we're excited to explore the integration of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) to create realistic dog breed images. This will not only expand our dataset but also offer users a unique feature to generate and interact with lifelike images of various breeds. As we venture into this new territory, we remain committed to ethical AI practices and the privacy of our users.

This Project was built using Python 3.12, Flask, JavaScript and Docker.
