The following results showcase the performance of our breed classification model, which utilizes a transfer learning approach with a pre-trained neural network. This model has been fine-tuned to recognize different dog breeds from images, applying various data augmentation techniques like rotation, width shifting, height shifting, and zooming to improve its robustness.

On the test dataset, the model achieved an accuracy of 83.53% across multiple breeds, demonstrating high precision and recall in several cases.
